Manali, Himachal Pradesh


Manali is a beautiful hill station nestled in the Himalayas, known for its snow-covered mountains, lush green valleys, and adventure sports. It's a perfect escape for nature lovers and thrill seekers alike.

Top 5 Attractions in Manali

  1. Solang Valley - Ideal for skiing and paragliding.
  2. Rohtang Pass - Famous for its breathtaking mountain views.
  3. Hidimba Devi Temple - An ancient temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Beas River - Perfect for white water rafting and nature walks.
  5. Old Manali - A peaceful village with bohemian vibes.

Must-Try Foods in Manali

  • Siddu - A local steamed bread stuffed with spiced fillings.
  • Thukpa - A spicy Tibetan noodle soup.
  • Trout Fish - Freshwater fish grilled with herbs.
  • Babru - Himachali version of stuffed puris with black gram.
  • Mittha - A sweet saffron rice dish with dry fruits.
